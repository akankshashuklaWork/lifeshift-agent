# 12-Week Relocation Timeline

## Weeks 1–2: Decide and research
- Set a target move date and relocation budget cap
- Shortlist 2–3 neighborhoods with commute and school constraints
- Compare cost of living vs current city

## Weeks 3–4: Housing and logistics
- Schedule virtual or in-person housing tours
- Get moving quotes (DIY vs professional movers)
- Notify employer/landlord and check lease break terms

## Weeks 5–8: Execute the move
- Book movers or truck rental
- Transfer utilities, internet, and insurance
- Update address with bank, IRS, employer, and medical records

## Weeks 9–12: Settle in
- Register vehicles and update driver's license if applicable
- Find primary care and pediatrician
- Join one local community group to build a support network

## Budget categories to mention
- Moving services and packing supplies
- Deposits, first/last month rent, broker fees
- Travel for house-hunting trips
- Furniture and home setup
- 10–15% contingency buffer
