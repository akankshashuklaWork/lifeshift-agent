# Retirement Planning Milestones

## Months 1–2: Inventory
- List all accounts, pensions, and beneficiary forms
- Estimate monthly spending in retirement (use current spend as baseline)
- Discuss retirement vision with partner or family

## Months 3–4: Stress-test
- Model healthcare costs before Medicare eligibility
- Identify income streams (Social Security, pension, investments, part-time work)
- Consider a phased retirement or consulting bridge

## Months 5–6: Lifestyle design
- Define weekly structure: health, social, purpose, travel
- Trial retirement budget for 1–3 months if possible
- Update estate documents with a professional

## Months 7–12: Execute
- Set official retirement date range
- Plan knowledge transfer at work
- Build social connections outside the workplace before leaving

## Non-financial priorities
- Purpose projects (volunteering, mentoring, hobbies)
- Healthcare and fitness routines
- Housing decisions (downsize, relocate, stay)
