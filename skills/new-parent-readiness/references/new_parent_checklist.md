# New Parent Readiness Checklist

## Before birth
- Confirm parental leave policy and dates with employer
- Choose pediatrician and hospital preference
- Build support roster (partner, family, friends, neighbors)
- Stock essentials for the first two weeks at home
- Prepare freezer meals and chore rotation schedule

## First month home
- Protect sleep windows for the primary caregiver
- Schedule pediatric follow-up appointments
- Set up a simple baby log for feeding and sleep (optional)

## Months 2–4
- Revisit budget for childcare, supplies, and medical copays
- Plan return-to-work transition if applicable
- Protect couple time — even 20 minutes daily helps

## Budget reminders
- Medical and birth-related costs
- Nursery gear and recurring supplies
- Childcare deposit or nanny share
- Parental leave income gap buffer
