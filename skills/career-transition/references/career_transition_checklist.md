# Career Transition Checklist

## Preparation phase
- Write a one-sentence positioning statement (who you help + what outcome)
- Audit transferable skills from current role to target role
- Update resume and LinkedIn with quantified achievements
- Identify 10 people to reconnect with for informational interviews
- Set weekly targets: applications, outreach messages, learning hours

## Transition phase
- Prepare 5 STAR stories for behavioral interviews
- Research salary bands for target role and location
- Plan notice period and knowledge transfer at current employer
- Block calendar for interview prep and skill-building

## Stabilization phase (first 90 days in new role)
- Define 30-60-90 day success metrics with manager
- Schedule weekly reflection on what's working
- Rebuild routines around new commute and schedule

## Networking cadence
- Week 1–2: Reach out to 3 warm contacts
- Week 3–4: Complete 2 informational interviews
- Week 5–8: Apply selectively to aligned roles, not volume spray
- Week 9–12: Interview prep and offer evaluation
